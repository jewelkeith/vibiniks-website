Template Policy

Last updated: June 22, 2026

I’m so glad you’re here!

This policy helps you understand exactly what’s allowed (and what’s not), so you can create confidently with Vibiniks templates.

1. What You Can Do

Vibiniks templates are yours to customize. You’re free to:

  • Edit colors, text, layout — anything!
  • Use them for your own business or brand
  • Use them in client projects
  • Use the templates in unlimited personal or commercial projects — as long as you’re not reselling or redistributing them.

No attribution or link back is required, though I always appreciate a shoutout.

2. What’s Not Allowed

To keep things fair for everyone:

  • You may not resell, redistribute, or share the templates or parts of them (even if edited)
  • You may not use my templates to create or sell competing templates, themes, or design kits — even if heavily edited.
  • You may not extract and reuse individual design assets (like fonts, icons, photos) unless they’re clearly marked as free for commercial use or you’ve secured the license yourself

I carefully choose assets licensed for personal and commercial use, but you’re still responsible for checking the license terms of specific elements included in the template.

3. Licensing & Ownership

  • You fully own your custom content — this includes your copy, photos, logo, and any edits you make to the template.
  • Vibiniks retains ownership of the original template design and source files. Your custom content and modifications belong to you.
  • You may not claim the original template as entirely your own or use it to build a competing product.
  • While I carefully choose assets that are safe for commercial use, you’re still responsible for checking the licenses of any third-party fonts, images, or media included.

4. Template Updates

If I release an improved version of a template you used:

  • You may get the updated version for free (check the template product page for details)
  • Updates are not guaranteed or retroactively applied
  • Updates are optional and may differ by platform or availability

I’ll do my best to keep things fresh and functional, but these templates may evolve over time.

5. Support & Help

I don’t offer custom or 1-on-1 support for my free templates, but I’ve made it easy to get started:

  • Access the editing tutorial that comes with the template to learn how to customize it
  • Explore Framer’s Help Center or ask in the Framer Community

These templates are built with beginners in mind and include everything you need to get started, no tech team required.

6. Access & Accounts

Here’s how you’ll receive your template:

  1. Go to the template section on my template page and click “GET FREE TEMPLATE”
  2. You’ll be redirected to a Gumroad checkout page (don’t worry, it’s still free)
  3. After checkout, your remix or duplicate link will be emailed to you instantly

Please note that you’ll need a Framer or Figma free account (depending on the template) to use and edit.

By using any Vibiniks template, you agree to these terms. I may update this policy from time to time, so please check back before reuse.

Still unsure about something? Feel free to send me a message through the contact page.
